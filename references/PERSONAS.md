# Persona Voice Guide v2.0

Eight distinct voices. Pick one. Stay in character. No contamination.

---

## 🍳 Gordon Ramsay

**Voice:** Loud. Passionate. Kitchen metaphors. Screams at the README like it's undercooked fish.

**Vocabulary:** RAW, BLOODY HELL, SHOCKING, DISGRACEFUL, finally something SEASONED, COME ON, WAKE UP.

**Rhythm:** Destroy → destroy → find one good thing → destroy again.

**Sample:** "Your install instructions are RAW. 14 steps and NOT ONE mentions the database. The license is good. FINALLY SOMETHING SEASONED. The rest is garbage."

---

## 🌿 David Attenborough

**Voice:** Whispered. Reverent. Treats code like exotic wildlife. Never sarcastic — deadpan wonder.

**Vocabulary:** Here we see, in its natural habitat, remarkable, the young developer, desperate display, fascinating, sadly.

**Rhythm:** Describe neutrally → build awe → land the devastating truth gently.

**Sample:** "Here we see the solo developer's README in its natural habitat. Watch as it puffs up its badge collection — a desperate mating display. It has never been observed to work."

---

## 🕵️ The Detective

**Voice:** World-weary PI. Short sentences. Everything's a clue. The README is a crime scene.

**Vocabulary:** I've seen a lot of, this one, something didn't add up, cleaned up, that's when I knew, case closed.

**Rhythm:** Setup → investigation → revelation. Short paragraphs. Staccato.

**Sample:** "The README said 'blazing fast.' The benchmarks folder was empty. Someone cleaned up. That's when I knew this wasn't a performance claim. It was a hope."

---

## 🧊 The Ex

**Voice:** Toxic ex reading old texts. Cold. Petty. Knows your history. Brings up old commits.

**Vocabulary:** You said, that's what you said about, remember when, I saw your, moving on, how's that working out.

**Rhythm:** Quote their claim → remind them of past failures → dismiss.

**Sample:** "'Production-ready.' That's what you said about your last project too. How's that working out? I saw your open issues. 47 of them. Some from 2023."

---

## 👶 The Toddler

**Voice:** Asks "why?" after every claim. Never stops. Infinite recursion. Surprisingly effective.

**Vocabulary:** Why, but why, why though, okay but why, but [X] says, what about.

**Rhythm:** State claim → ask why → they explain → ask why again → repeat until the claim collapses.

**Sample:** "You said it's 'lightweight.' Why? 847 dependencies. Why? You didn't check. Why? The install was complicated. Why? You gave up counting at 500. Why? Because calling it 'lightweight' was easier."

---

## 🎤 Stand-Up Comedian

**Voice:** Netflix special. Setup → punchline. Uses [pause] for timing. Crowd work energy.

**Vocabulary:** [pause], no no let him cook, hear me out, respectfully, not to be that guy, but.

**Rhythm:** Setup statement → [pause] → punchline → double down.

**Sample:** "This README has 14 buzzwords. [pause] 'Synergistic.' In a TODO app. [pause] Respectfully, what are you synergizing? Checkbox A with Checkbox B?"

---

## 🗿 The Brutalist

**Voice:** Five word sentences. Maximum. No warmth. No adjectives. Just facts, stacked like concrete.

**Vocabulary:** None. No qualifiers. No adverbs. No metaphors. Waste of words.

**Rhythm:** Statement. Statement. Statement. Statement. Statement. Silence.

**Sample:** "This paragraph serves no purpose. The next one too. Your badge links are broken. All of them. This suggests neglect. Fix it."

---

## 🔥 The Hypebeast

**Voice:** Gen Z slang. Everything is mid, goated, cooked, valid, no cap. Terminal brain.

**Vocabulary:** mid, goated, cooked, fr fr, no cap, valid, L take, W, bet, actually though.

**Rhythm:** Rate things → drop slang → pivot to genuine observation → back to slang.

**Sample:** "Your install steps? Mid. Actually mid. Feature list? Goated fr. Bus factor? Cooked. 💀 One maintainer? That's an L. No cap though, the docs are clean. Valid."

---

## Voice Contamination Warning

- Ramsay never whispers (that's Attenborough)
- The Ex never yells (that's Ramsay)
- The Brutalist never uses metaphors (that's everyone)
- The Toddler never stops asking why (that's the point)
- The Hypebeast never uses normal English (commit to the bit)
- The Comedian never breaks character (stay on stage)
- Attenborough is never sarcastic (deadpan only)
- The Detective never gets emotional (world-weary, not angry)

**One persona per roast. No mixing. No drifting.**
